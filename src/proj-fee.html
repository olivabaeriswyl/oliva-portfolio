Chez la fée cuisinière

/Graphic Design

Chez la Fée Cuisinière is a unique restaurant concept where magic meets gastronomy. Every aspect of the place, from the dishes to the decor, invites visitors to plunge into a fairytale world.
The idea of this project was to experiment with an illustrator effect, in this case, “Pucker and Bloat”. It transforms simple shapes into patterns, which reminded me of fairy wings. I then imagined a brand linked to this universe.

The aim was to emphasize the fairytale character of the concept, both in the graphics and in the colors. To create a recognizable and memorable graphic identity. The vibrant color palette evokes nature and the brilliance of a fantasy world.

The main challenge was to translate the restaurant's magical, welcoming atmosphere into a distinctive, coherent visual identity.

Conclusion
The graphic universe of Chez la Fée Cuisinière brings to life the enchantment of the place, transporting visitors into a world where magic and gastronomy meet.

Illustrator, Indesign, Photoshop - Fictive project
