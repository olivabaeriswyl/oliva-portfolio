L'heure du thé

/Graphic Design

/Motion Design

This project involved creating a graphic and informative page highlighting facts about tea.
The choice of geometric shapes such as rounded petals and angular stems creates a visual structure that is both playful and orderly. Each graphic element is designed to facilitate understanding while capturing the viewer's attention.

The goal was to translate numbers and graphs into visually appealing and coherent elements, maintain a balance between graphic creativity and data legibility, and create a design that evoked the world of tea while being modern and abstract. I also researched tea and computer graphics design.

Conclusion
This project enabled me to combine graphic design and visual communication to simplify complex data.
