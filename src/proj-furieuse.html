Website link

The objective of this project was to promote the Furieuse comic strip through an immersive digital experience, combining an interactive website and a narrative video.
Rather than a classic presentation, the idea was to plunge the user directly into the world of the comic strip, adopting the point of view of the Baron, an important character.

I conceived the storytelling for the website, the Baron's monologue. I also produced a proof of concept for horizontal scrolling and parallax to learn about these effects, which I'd never used before.

Conclusion
This promotion is a journey into the world of comics. It brings the world of Furieuse to life with an animated website and video.
